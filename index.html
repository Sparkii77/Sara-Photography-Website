Welcome

Hi, my name is Sara and welcome to my gallery!

I would like to classify myself as an amateur photographer, coder and chocolate critic. This website contains a little of what goes on in my head and hopefully, you find it interesting too.

In this site, there are various galleries of images I've taken over the years based on some of my favourites themes.

To locate back to the homepage, please press the icon at the far left corner. Hover over the images and click on them to have a closer look.

I hope you enjoy looking through them and even maybe get inspired!
